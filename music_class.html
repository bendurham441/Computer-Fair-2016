Mrs. Benton

  • B.M., Music Education, University of Delaware
  • M.M., Music Education, Temple University

Mrs. Benton teaches general music at the middle school (grades 6-8), directs both the 6th grade and 7th/8th grade chorus, and serves as the director of the drama productions. When she is not teaching, Mrs. Benton enjoys singing, going to see musical performances and shows, running, and spending time with family, especially her husband Rob and her cat, Maddox.

About the Music Program

At STMS, students learn about music in many different ways, including learning how to play on keyboards as well as composing their own music on online software. Music Class is a marking period special, which means that students have music every day for a quarter of the year.
